Hometown

Surabaya, Indonesia


Surabaya the city of heroes, this city got it name after that heroic incident of 10th November causing the victory of "pribumi" over colonizer. The city of Surabaya is located on the northern coast of East Java, and is directly adjacent to the Madura Strait in the north and east, Sidoarjo Regency in the south, and Gresik Regency in the west. The city of Surabaya is located in the lowlands, with an altitude of between 3-6 M above sea level, this also causes the air temperature in Surabaya to be relatively hot and dry. Surabaya has become a metropolis with some rich diversity in it. In addition, Surabaya has now also become a center of business, trade, industry, and education in Indonesia. Surabaya is the main port and center of commercial trade in eastern Indonesia, and is now one of the largest cities in Southeast Asia. Together with Lamongan in the northwest, Gresik in the west, Bangkalan in the northeast, Sidoarjo in the south, Mojokerto and Jombang in the southwest, it is a unit called Gerbang Kertosusila, like Jabodetabek in Jakarta and its surroundings.
